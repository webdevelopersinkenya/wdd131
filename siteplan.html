Kenya's Wildlife - Website Planning Document

Logo Idea: A simple silhouette of an elephant and acacia tree with the site name underneath.

Site Name:

Kenya’s Wildlife Wonders

This name reflects the richness of Kenya’s wild animals, landscapes, and conservation story. It captures the beauty and wonder that the site will showcase.

Optional Domain: kenyaswildlifewonders.org

Site Purpose:

The purpose of this site is to introduce visitors to Kenya’s wildlife by providing information on iconic animals, national parks, and conservation efforts. It also serves as a resource for tourists and nature lovers who want to learn more about safaris and wildlife preservation in Kenya.

Scenarios:

  • Which animals are most commonly seen on a Kenyan safari?
  • What are the top national parks to visit in Kenya and what makes each unique?
  • How is Kenya protecting endangered species like elephants and rhinos?

Color Schema:

The site will use a natural color palette inspired by the savannah.

  • Dark Green (#2C5F2D) – used for headings and accents.
  • Golden Yellow (#E8B923) – used for navigation and highlights.
  • Light Gray (#F4F4F4) – used as background color.

Typography:

  • Roboto – used for body text for readability.
  • Merriweather – used for headings to add elegance and contrast.
